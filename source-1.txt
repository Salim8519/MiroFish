1. Tartan (football) fields ⚽
It is one of the most requested places for daily or weekly reservations among young people to organize six- and seven-player football matches.

Locations: It is widely spread in all states of Muscat (Seeb, Maabilah, Al Khoud, Bawshar, and Al Amerat).

Examples: Oman Automobile Association stadiums, and many private stadiums in neighborhoods.

How to book: Many of them are now easily booked through specialized local applications such as the “Midan” application, which allows knowing the available times and dividing the payment between the players.

2. Padel courts 🎾
The fastest growing and popular sport in Muscat currently. It combines tennis and squash and requires advance reservation due to the high demand.

Locations:

Wave Padel

Qurum Courts

There are also courts available in specialized clubs in Muscat. They can often be booked via the Meydan app or by direct contact.

3. Gaming and PlayStation halls 🎮
Places equipped with giant screens, PS5 devices, and PC Gaming computers with high specifications, in addition to private rooms (VIP) that can be booked by the hour for gatherings of friends.

Locations: * Private PlayStation rooms at the Fun Zone Center in Qurum.

There are many specialized gaming cafes (Gaming Lounges) spread in the Al Khoud and Al Mabilah areas.

4. Escape Rooms 🔐
An experience based on intelligence and teamwork. The room is reserved for your team (usually 2-10 people), and you have 60 minutes to solve the puzzles and escape the room.

Location: * Escape Muscat Adventures (Xcape Muscat): It is considered the first of its kind in the Sultanate. It offers real escape rooms and virtual reality (VR) escape rooms. They have branches on Muzoon Street (Al Khoudh) and on the ground floor of Muscat Mall (Maabilah).

5. Trampoline Parks 🤸‍♂️
Huge indoor halls containing vast areas of connected trampolines, climbing walls, and Ninja Courses. Excellent for releasing energy for adults and children.

Locations:

Trampo Extreme: One of the most popular destinations, with locations in Ghala Heights and Al Araimi Boulevard.

6. Go-Karting tracks 🏎️
Book go-karts and experience the adrenaline and speed of a professional track.

Locations:

Muscat Speedway: Located in the Oman Automobile Association in Seeb. It provides a 1.2 km track and a professional driving experience for children and adults.

7. Bowling alleys 🎳
A classic and very fun option for gatherings, you can reserve a lane for you and your friends to enjoy playing while eating snacks.

Locations:

Fun Zone Center: In Qurum, it offers a wonderful bowling alley.

Cosmic Bowling: A luxurious experience at the Kempinski Hotel (Al Mouj Muscat).

Ground Control: In Mall of Oman (third floor).

8. Laser Tag Arenas 🔫
A safe combat game that takes place in dark rooms filled with smoke and neon lighting, where you divide into teams and use laser guns to score points.

Locations:

Lasermaxx: Located inside the Fun Zone center in Qurum, it accommodates up to 14 players per session.

9. Board Game Cafes 🎲
A relatively new concept in Muscat but very popular. You can reserve a table to sit for hours with friends and play complex and fun board games like Dungeons & Dragons, Monopoly, and more.

Locations:

The Hearth Café: Located in Muscat, it is the first café specializing in board games. It offers a calm atmosphere with drinks named “magic potions” to match the gaming atmosphere.

10. Ice Skating ⛸️
Places that allow you to reserve a time (usually 60 minutes) to freestyle on the ice with friends.

Locations:

Ice skating rink at Fun Zone Center in Crimea.
